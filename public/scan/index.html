How it works

1

Tags go on equipment

Small NFC pucks stick to any gym machine, rack, bench, or wall. Each tag pairs to a piece of equipment once — every user benefits forever.

2

Tap your phone

Hold your iPhone near a puck. iOS shows a banner — tap it. RYZN opens, the right lift is pre-loaded with your last weights, your sets and reps already filled in.

3

Tap the next one

Between exercises, tap the next machine. Set, rep, weight — all auto-filled. The whole workout builds itself as you move through the gym.
