How it works

1

Tags go on equipment

Small NFC pucks stick on machines, racks, mats, anywhere you train. Each tag pairs to an exercise once — every user benefits forever.

2

Tap your phone

Hold your iPhone to the puck. iOS shows a banner — tap it. RYZN opens, the right exercise is pre-loaded with your last weights.

3

Tap the next one

Between exercises, tap the next machine. Set, rep, weight, all auto-filled. The whole workout builds itself as you move through the gym.

Why it matters

Zero typing

Stop fumbling with picker lists between sets. Your gym becomes the interface.

Last weights, ready

RYZN pre-fills the bar from your last session on that exercise. No guessing what you did last week.

Works in any gym

Tags pair once, work for every RYZN user who scans them. Your home gym, a hotel, a chain — the system follows.

No batteries

NFC pucks are passive. No charging, no replacing. Stick once, last a decade.
