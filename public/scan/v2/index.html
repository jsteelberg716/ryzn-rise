The handshake

From the moment your phone gets within an inch, here's what happens in under a second.

T+0ms

Field on. Your iPhone emits a 13.56 MHz radio field looking for a tag.

T+80ms

Coil energized. The puck's copper antenna picks up energy and wakes up.

T+200ms

URL sent. The puck broadcasts the link ryznrise.com/scan.

T+450ms

Sheet shown. iOS surfaces the URL preview at the bottom of your screen.

T+<1s
